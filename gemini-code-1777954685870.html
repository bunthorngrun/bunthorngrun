Bunthorng Run

Mathematics Graduate

Bunthorng is a Mathematician and graduate of the Budapest University of Technology and Economics (BME). As a former Stipendium Hungaricum scholar from Cambodia, he has built a strong academic foundation in Central Europe, focusing on the intersection of theoretical logic and complex geometric systems.

Bachelor's Thesis "Equioptic and Apollonius Circles in Hyperbolic Geometry"

His research delves into the intricate properties of hyperbolic space, demonstrating a high level of analytical rigor and mathematical intuition. Beyond the classroom, Bunthorng has actively expanded his professional and cultural horizons by participating in Erasmus+ projects in Poland and Portugal, where he collaborated on international initiatives and developed a global perspective on education and technology.

Driven by a passion for community building and lifelong learning, Bunthorng combines his mathematical expertise with a commitment to social impact. He is particularly interested in how the precise logic of geometry and optimization can be applied to solve real-world challenges, both in Cambodia and on the international stage.
